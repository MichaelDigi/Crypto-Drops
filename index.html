What is an NFT?

Abbreviation for: (non-fungible token)

NFT stands for non-fungible token. An NFT is a unit of data – which can represent art, photos, videos, audio and other types of digital files – stored on a digital ledger, called a blockchain. NFTs are certified digital assets that are unique and therefore not interchangeable.
